Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the show?

The show is designed for 90 minutes but I do not have a time limit. I don't rush shows to make sure guest are satisfied.

How far do you travel?

I only travel outside the Charleston area to towns like Mt. Pleasant, Folly Beach, Isle of Palms, and even Summerville, SC. Call to see if we can make arrangements.

Can I do an early show?

Yes, day time, brunch show, midday, afternoon or late night, I can even do a 9 am show for you.

You will pay me for the service when I arrive at your party. Flat rate no matter the length. VIP, hot seats and ect. included in flat price. I dont add on fees or require mandatory tip outs.
